## ðŸ“ Recipe System

Every dish becomes a **recipe**. Every recipe becomes a **story**. Every story becomes part of The Imaginatorium's narrative.

### Recipe Creation:
```
[2025-11-22T12:00:00Z|cook|vdamo|kitchen|recipe:muffin-mexicana]{
  name:"Muffin Mexicana";
  ingredients:"english-muffin,cheese,bacon,tomato,avocado,taco-mince";
  steps:"toast-muffin,add-cheese,add-bacon,add-tomato,add-avocado,add-taco-mince";
  dietary:"meat,gluten";
  mood:"experimental,celebratory"
}
```

This single CML entry contains everything needed to:
- **Render as Recipe Card** - Full recipe with ingredients and instructions
- **Add to Menu** - Weekly/monthly menu planning
- **Generate Shopping List** - Automatic ingredient lists
- **Filter by Dietary** - Vegetarian, vegan, keto, gluten-free options
- **Tell the Story** - How the recipe was created, who enjoyed it
