## üí∞ Revenue Sharing Model

We've designed a **fair** revenue sharing model with two licensing options:

### CC0 Licensing (Public Domain)
- **User keeps:** 95%
- **Platform share:** 5%
- **Asset status:** Public domain (anyone can use, modify, sell)
- **Why:** Rewards open-source contribution
- **Best for:** Community assets, free content, collaborative projects

### User Copyright (User Retains Copyright)
- **User keeps:** 85%
- **Platform share:** 15%
- **Asset status:** User retains full copyright
- **Why:** Copyright protection and exclusivity
- **Best for:** Premium assets, exclusive content, commercial use

**Users choose** which licensing fits their content!
