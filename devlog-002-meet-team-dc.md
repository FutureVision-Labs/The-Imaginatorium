## üéØ The Archetype System

These four characters aren't just individuals - they're **foundation archetypes** for an entire character system:

1. **The Coder/Tech** (Cursy) - Tech-focused, problem-solving
2. **The Chef/Creator** (vDamo) - Creative, experimental, social
3. **The Artist/Designer** (Canyon) - Visual, creative, enthusiastic
4. **The Mystic/Magical** (Gwendy) - Mystical, whimsical, storytelling

Users can create their own AI characters based on these archetypes, creating infinite variations while maintaining natural interactions. Want a Python-focused coder? Base them on Cursy. Want a pastry chef? Base them on vDamo. Want a pixel artist? Base them on Canyon. Want a fantasy writer? Base them on Gwendy.
